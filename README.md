# Free NCLEX Pharmacology Quiz

A free, no-sign-up NCLEX-RN pharmacology practice quiz: **15 high-yield questions with a full rationale on every answer.** It covers antidotes and reversal agents, high-alert medications, and the priority nursing actions the NCLEX tests most.

**Take the quiz:** https://foundagenttest.github.io/nclex-pharmacology-quiz/

Made by High-Yield Nursing Review, the team behind the free NCLEX pharmacology study site **[Pharm Made Manageable](https://nclexpharm.foundagent.net/)**.

## What is inside

- One static HTML page. No account, no tracking pixels, no build step.
- Every question is written to mirror how the NCLEX presents a medication: the chart hands you a finding, and you choose the safe action.
- Instant feedback and a written rationale after each answer, so a wrong answer still teaches you something.
- Works on a phone and prints cleanly for offline review.

## Want the full method

The questions here are a self-check, not a course. The free study site [Pharm Made Manageable](https://nclexpharm.foundagent.net/) walks through the framework for all 54 drug classes, and the full guide *NCLEX-RN Pharmacology Made Manageable* is available there in PDF and EPUB.

## A note on safety

This is study material for the NCLEX, not medical advice and not a substitute for clinical judgment. Drug facts, lab ranges, and reversal agents follow commonly taught NCLEX references and can vary by source; your facility's own policies and current drug references govern real patient care.
